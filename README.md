# Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS Research Project

## Objective

The goal of this project was to research leading experts in B2B SaaS cold outreach and outbound sales. I collected LinkedIn content, YouTube learning materials, and additional resources to identify best practices for building pipeline and improving outbound performance.

## Experts Researched

1. Josh Braun
2. Jason Bay
3. Becc Holland
4. Morgan J Ingram
5. Kyle Coleman
6. Trish Bertuzzi
7. John Barrows
8. Will Aitken
9. Sangram Vajre
10. Additional supporting resources

## Repository Structure

* research/sources.md
* research/linkedin-posts/
* research/youtube-transcripts/
* research/other/

## Key Learnings

* Personalization is more effective than mass outreach.
* Strong prospecting habits drive pipeline growth.
* Cold calling remains a valuable outbound channel.
* AI should support, not replace, sales fundamentals.
* Sales success comes from consistency and customer understanding.

## Why These Experts

I selected these experts because they actively share practical advice on outbound sales, prospecting, pipeline generation, and B2B SaaS growth. Their content is widely followed by sales professionals and revenue teams.
