Summary:
In this discussion, Trish Bertuzzi shares her expertise on building effective sales development organizations and creating scalable outbound programs. She explains the importance of defining clear SDR responsibilities, aligning sales and marketing efforts, and creating repeatable processes for pipeline generation. The conversation also covers hiring, coaching, and performance management for SDR teams.

Key Insights:

* Successful SDR teams operate with clearly defined roles and responsibilities.
* Alignment between sales and marketing is critical for pipeline generation.
* Consistent processes create predictable outbound results.
* Coaching and development are essential for SDR success.
* Metrics should focus on quality outcomes, not just activity volume.

Why It Matters:
Trish Bertuzzi is recognized as one of the pioneers of modern sales development. Her frameworks have influenced how many B2B SaaS companies structure SDR teams, build outbound motions, and scale revenue generation.
